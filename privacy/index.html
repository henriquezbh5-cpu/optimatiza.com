Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ClickMental AI ("the Service", "we", "our"), operated by Humberto Henriquez, collects, uses, and protects information when you authorize the Service to publish content to your social media accounts on your behalf.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize ClickMental AI through a third-party platform (such as TikTok) using OAuth 2.0, we receive:

  • Account identifier (e.g., TikTok open_id) — a platform-issued identifier used to associate uploads with your account.
  • Display name and avatar URL — used only to show which account you are currently connected to.
  • Access and refresh tokens — scoped credentials that allow the Service to publish videos you explicitly queue for upload.

We do not collect your password, private messages, email, or any personal data beyond what is strictly required to publish content you have approved.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To upload and publish video content that you have created and explicitly scheduled via the Service.
  • To refresh expired access tokens automatically so you do not need to re-authenticate frequently.
  • To keep a local log of successful and failed upload attempts for troubleshooting purposes.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the publishing workflow you initiated.

3. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens and account identifiers are stored locally on the device where ClickMental AI is installed. Tokens are stored in a file protected by the operating system's file-level permissions. We do not transmit tokens to any third-party server operated by us, and we do not maintain a centralized database of user credentials.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. The only external service that receives your tokens is the platform that issued them (e.g., TikTok), and only for the purpose of completing the API calls required to publish your content.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Tokens and account information are retained locally until:

  • You revoke authorization on the platform (e.g., TikTok Settings → Manage apps).
  • You manually delete the local token file.
  • You uninstall the Service.

Upload logs are retained locally for a maximum of 90 days, after which they are automatically rotated.

6. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke the Service's access from your TikTok account settings.
  • Request deletion of any locally stored data by contacting us.
  • Request a copy of any data the Service holds about you.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or requests related to your data can be sent through the contact form on our homepage.
